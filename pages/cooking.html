High Protein Creamy Breakfast Smoothie With 48g of Protein

Ingredients

1 banana

80g blueberry

25g protein granola / granola

50g greek yogurt

20g peanut butter

30g protein powder

200ml fat-free or low-fat milk

Steps

Blender

Add the banana

Add the blueberries

Add the cup of granola

Add the teaspoon of greek yogurt

Add the spoon of peanut butter

Add the scoop of peanut butter

Add the milk
